About Us

Penguin Random House India is the largest English language trade publisher in the subcontinent, publishing over 250 new titles every year and with an active backlist of over 3000 titles.

Founded in 1985, Penguin India's prestigious list includes Booker Prize winning titles, the Penguin Classics and a range of works by Nobel laureates. We are home to some of the most acclaimed writers from the Indian subcontinent.

Our mission is to foster a universal passion for reading by creating books for everyone to enjoy. We believe that books, and the stories they contain, have the unique capacity to connect us as individuals.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my manuscript?

We accept manuscript submissions via email at submissions@penguinrandomhouse.in. Please include a synopsis, the first three chapters, and your author bio.

Do you offer internships or job opportunities?

Yes, we regularly offer internships and job opportunities. Please visit our Careers page or email us at careers@penguinrandomhouse.in for more information.

How can I request review copies?

For review copies, please send your request along with your media credentials to media@penguinrandomhouse.in. Please allow 2-3 weeks for processing.

Can I visit your office?

Our office is open for scheduled visits only. Please contact us via email or phone to arrange an appointment at least 48 hours in advance.
