About the Authors

Subah Saraf and Harshvardhan Saraf are the founders of the Satvic Movement, a non-profit health education platform dedicated to reconnecting people with the power of nature for holistic healing. Driven by their own personal health journeys and a deep understanding of natural wellness principles, they have inspired millions across the globe to adopt a healthier, more conscious lifestyle.

Through workshops, online courses, and insightful content, Subah and Harshvardhan share practical, evidence-based knowledge on food, detoxification, and natural living. Their mission is to empower individuals to take charge of their health and experience the profound benefits of aligning with nature's laws. The Satvic Revolution encapsulates their core teachings, making this life-changing wisdom accessible to all.
